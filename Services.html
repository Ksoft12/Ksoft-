Our Services

At KSOFT, our multi-skilled teams deliver process and design solutions using industry-leading platforms like Siemens, Rockwell, Delta and Mitsubishi, across the globe. We offer premium quality PLC and SCADA implementation resource services. KSOFT industrial automation service offerings include interfacing factory lines with intelligent systems, such as recipe management systems, reporting and productivity monitoring systems, databases, FIS(Factory Information System), MPTS(Multi-Protocol Transport Services), OMS(Operations Management System) and MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems). KSOFT offers factory automation, integration, and programming services from our office in Sangli,Maharashta (India) to customers worldwide. We offer a range of automation engineering services tailored to meet the needs of various industries. Explore our service offerings below to find out how we can help enhance your operations.
